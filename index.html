Research Interests

My research focuses on elucidating the etiology of cardiometabolic diseases by integrating multi-omics data (proteomics, metabolomics) within large-scale population cohorts. I employ Mendelian randomization and other causal inference methods to identify and validate novel biomarkers for disease risk prediction, aiming to inform early prevention and precision public health strategies.
